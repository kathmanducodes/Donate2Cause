About Us

We believe in the power of community to transform lives. Donate2Cause bridges the gap between those who need help and those who want to help.

Our Mission

At Donate2Cause, we are committed to creating a transparent, secure, and accessible platform that connects donors with verified causes across Nepal.

We aim to rebuild trust in charitable giving by implementing rigorous verification processes, ensuring every rupee donated makes a real impact.

Our platform empowers families in need, enables the diaspora to support their homeland, and fosters a culture of transparent giving in Nepal.
